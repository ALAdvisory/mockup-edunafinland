### Finnish Special Education Framework

**Section Headline:** Finnish Special Education Framework

**Introduction:** These principles shaped decades of successful inclusive education in Finland. They work.

**Principles:**

1. **Learning Through Play & Curiosity**
   Children explore and discover through joyful, hands-on experiences that spark natural curiosity.

2. **All-Day Pedagogy**
   Learning happens everywhere — not only in lessons, but in play, routines, and relationships.

3. **Equality & Inclusion as Foundation**
   Every child deserves the same opportunities and support to thrive, regardless of their needs.

4. **Collaboration & Reflection**
   Teachers work together, share insights, and continuously reflect on their practice.

5. **Trust in Teachers**
   Educators are respected professionals with the autonomy to make thoughtful decisions.

6. **Shared Responsibility**
   Education is a collaborative effort between teachers, care givers, and the community.
